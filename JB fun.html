Who is Justin Biber?

Canadian singer Justin Bieber has released six studio albums, five compilations and remixes, two mini-albums, He became famous thanks to the videos that his mother posted on YouTube. On November 17, 2009, the debut mini-album My World and the studio album My World 2.0, a continuation of the first part of the album, were released. The first part of the album took the fifth place in the Billboard 200 chart, and the second part debuted at the first place of the chart. Seven singles were released from the albums, two — My World and five — My World 2.0.

Justin's second studio album, Under the Mistletoe , was released on November 1, 2011. The lead single "Mistletoe" reached the eleventh place in the Hot 100. Justin's third album, Believe, was released in mid—July 2012. The lead single was the song "Boyfriend". Five singles were released from the album. The album reached number one on the Billboard 200 chart.

Progress

Throughout his career, Bieber has sold about 100 million records, making him the best-selling male Canadian musician and one of the best-selling music artists in the world. On November 23, 2012, Bieber was awarded the Queen Elizabeth II Diamond Jubilee Medal by Canadian Prime Minister Stephen Harper. In 2013, Bieber received a Diamond certification award from the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA) for his single "Baby", which became the highest certified digital song of all time.Bieber has six nominations and one Grammy Award win, two BRIT Awards, five NRJ Music Awards, fourteen awards from the Billboard Music Awards. With the participation of his fans, he received twelve wins at the American Music Awards and twenty-three at the Teen Choice Awards. He also won twenty-one MTV Europe Music Awards, which is a record for one musician. As of 2016, Bieber holds fourteen Guinness World Records, including eight that were achieved thanks to the success of his album Purpose.
